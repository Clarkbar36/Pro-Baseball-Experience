McBash, Florida Start Round 1 Strong with Home Win
The Florida Space Rangers started the Round 1 in style today, downing the visiting San Antonio Sloths at USSSA Space Coast Coliseum by a score of 7-6 to stake an early 1-0 lead in the best-of-5 series. Space Rangers third baseman George McBash was named player of the game after he was 2 for 3 with 2 home runs and a walk. He scored 3 runs and drove in 4.

Nate Piazza had a clutch at-bat with one out in the bottom of the sixth for the Space Rangers. Coming up with the bases loaded and Florida trailing 6-5, he hit a 2-run single.

"It was a solid team effort and we'll try to keep this momentum going," Piazza told reporters after the game.

Game 2 is set to be played tomorrow in Providence. The scheduled starters for the game are Khamati Kahn for San Antonio and D'Aroldis Borkman for Florida.
