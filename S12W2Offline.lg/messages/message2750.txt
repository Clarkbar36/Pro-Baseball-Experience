Playoffs? No Chance for Vancouver
The <Vancouver Vandals:team#11> have spent much of the season on the outside looking in at the playoff race. They will be permanently on the outside now, after being officially eliminated from contention today.

This is the club's 5th playoff miss in a row. They last appeared in the postseason in 2022. In the last 10 seasons, the Vandals have missed the playoffs 5 times. In total, the Vandals have missed the playoffs 5 times in their 10-year history. Vancouver has won the World Series 5 times in their history.
