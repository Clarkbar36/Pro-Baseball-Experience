PBE Player of the Week, DeMars of Florida
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. <John DeMars:player#379>, the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> 24-year-old left fielder, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 10 hits in 16 at-bats, compiling a .625 average with 3 home runs and 7 RBIs.

In 37 games this season <DeMars:player#379> has compiled a .329 batting average with 46 hits and 6 home runs. He has also scored 20 times and knocked in 24 runs.
