PBE News: Spring Training Begins
The 2028 Pro Baseball Experience spring training facilities will soon be opening and ablaze with activity. It is a time of hope and heartbreak. A time for ballplayers to get in shape, hone their talents and showcase their skills -- to be all they can be. All under the watchful eye of the front office, whose job it is to make the crucial decisions that will affect not only this upcoming season, but future seasons as well. Who goes to the big time and who goes back to the bushes? That is what spring training is all about.
