PBE Player of the Week, Kittone of Death Valley
At the age of 27, <Catolo Kittone:player#158> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. This year <Kittone:player#158> has hit at a .323 clip with 90 hits, 17 home runs, 69 RBIs and 54 runs scored.
