Min-joon Do Wins PBE Trophy
At the age of 30, <Min-joon Do:player#239> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Death Valley:team#10> second baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .467 batting average. For his efforts <Do:player#239> collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 7 hits in 15 at-bats, 3 home runs and 9 RBIs last week in 4 games.

This year <Do:player#239> has socked 76 hits in 256 at-bats for a .297 average with 20 home runs. He is getting on base at a .405 pace and his totals also include 67 RBIs and 56 runs scored.
