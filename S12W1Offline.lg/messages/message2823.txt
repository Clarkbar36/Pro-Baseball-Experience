Best PBE Reliever Named
<Frederick Birdington:player#368> of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> said he never set out to win the Pro Baseball Experience Reliever of the Year Award this season, but he'll certainly take it.

"2027 was a pretty good year for me, that much is obvious," he said. "This is icing on the cake."

The 27-year-old out of Exeter, New Hampshire fashioned a 3.14 ERA this season over 100.1 innings of work. He made 60 relief appearances and had 15 saves, 101 strikeouts, 36 walks and a record of 8 wins and 2 losses.

<JJ Windsor:player#336> of the <Outer Banks Aviators:team#2> finished second in voting, while <Trent Boult:player#43> of the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> finished third.
