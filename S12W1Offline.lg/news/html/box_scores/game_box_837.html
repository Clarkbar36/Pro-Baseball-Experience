Road Win Lifts Anchorage to 2-1 Lead in RF
The Anchorage Wheelers moved to within one game of the Round 2 today, downing the State College Swift Steeds on the road at Ballpark of Meeker by a score of 10-5. The win gives the Wheelers a 2-1 advantage in the best-of-5 Rookies Finals.

Anchorage grabbed control in the top of the eighth. With one out and runners on 1st and 3rd, Jack Bauer II delivered a run-scoring single, to make it 6-5 in favor of the Wheelers.

"It was a solid team effort," said Bauer II after the game.
