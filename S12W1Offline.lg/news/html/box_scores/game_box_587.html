McSlapp Slugs Swift Steeds to 10-3 Win
The State College Swift Steeds knocked off the Anchorage Wheelers, 10-3, at Balto Memorial Field at The Igloo. Mike McSlapp, who captured player of the game honors, was a big reason State College won. The right fielder went 4 for 5 with 2 home runs and a double. He drove in 6 runs and scored twice. The first-place Swift Steeds have their sights set on making a deep playoff run.

With State College leading 1-0 in the top of the second, McSlapp hit a fastball from Amp Dupree for a 2-run double. It was his 10th double of the season and put the Swift Steeds ahead, 3-0.

"Nice to put this one in the win column," said McSlapp.
