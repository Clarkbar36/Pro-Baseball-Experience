Vandals Continue Skid Thanks to McBash, Space Rangers
The Florida Space Rangers downed the Vancouver Vandals, 5-3, at Vandal Dome. The Space Rangers were led by George McBash, who captured player of the game honors. He hit 3 home runs, while adding 4 RBI and scoring three times. Barry Keller, the winner, improved to 2-0. He held the Vandals to 1 earned run on 5 hits over 6 innings, while striking out 6 and walking 2. Trent Boult picked up the save for Florida, his 3rd of the year.

McBash had a game-changing home run in the top of the sixth. On a 2-2 knuckleball from Hayley MaConnell, he banged a 2-run home run to left, giving the Space Rangers a 3-2 lead. It was the 3rd home run of the year for McBash.

McBash called the triumph "a good team win."

The Space Rangers, now 6-1, have stormed out of the gates.
