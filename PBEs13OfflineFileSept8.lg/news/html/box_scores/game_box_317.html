The Wheelers, with just their 2nd win so far in 2029, have yet to catch fire.

Grayson Taylor was the walk-off hero for Anchorage. With the game tied in the bottom of the ninth inning, the Wheelers center fielder hit a dramatic run-scoring single to send the teams home.

Drumbo said this kind of win gives the team "a buzz."
