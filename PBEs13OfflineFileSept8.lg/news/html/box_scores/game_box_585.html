The Anchorage Wheelers beat the State College Swift Steeds , 8-6, at Balto Memorial Field at The Igloo. The win was credited to Thomas Kane III in relief. His record improves to 1-3. Bruce Quick took the loss. Jason Smith earned the save, his 5th.

Hilmer contributed a big hit, a 2-run home run, for Anchorage in the bottom of the first. With his team trailing 1-0, Hilmer took a fastball from Bruce Quick and stroked the home run to right-center. His 5th home run of the season allowed the Wheelers to leap in front, 2-1.

"We got the victory," said Hilmer. "That's all that matters at the end of the day."

Anchorage is now riding a 3-game win streak.
