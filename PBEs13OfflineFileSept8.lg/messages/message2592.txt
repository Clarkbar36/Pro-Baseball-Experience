PBE Selects Fernandez May's Best Pitcher
<Felipe Fernandez:player#218> was the anchor in the <San Antonio:team#9> bullpen last month and snatched up the Pro Baseball Experience Pitcher of the Month honors for May. The 30-year-old is in the prime of his career and pitching like he intends to stay around baseball for quite some time to come.
