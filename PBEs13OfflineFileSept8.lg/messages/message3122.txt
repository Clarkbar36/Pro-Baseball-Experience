New York Takes Round 1 in Deciding Game
The <New York Voyagers:team#1> are heading to the World Series, having narrowly defeated the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24>.

New York edged Florida in the series 3-2, with a win at Voyager Stadium in the clinching game by a 6-2 margin.

<Eduardo Dinero:player#81> turned in a strong offensive performance for the Voyagers and got series MVP for his efforts.

"I'll never turn down another trophy for my mantle, another accomplishment to remember once my career is over," he said. "What I would really remember, though, is a World Series win."

It remains to be seen how the playoffs will conclude, but if Dinero continues to swing a hot bat, his team will no doubt have a chance.

For the series, he hit .263 with a .300 on-base percentage. He had 3 home runs, 9 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The <New York Voyagers:team#1> will play against the <San Antonio Sloths:team#9> in the next round. San Antonio won their series against the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> 3 games to 1. The World Series schedule will be announced after the Round 1 finishes.
