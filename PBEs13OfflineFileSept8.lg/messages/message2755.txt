Faux Wins MiLPBE Top Pitcher Honors 
<Blake Faux:player#481> took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Minor League PBE Pitcher of the Month award for September.

The 21-year-old <Swift Steeds :team#18> starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 4-1 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 28 batters in 44.1 innings and compiled a 3.86 ERA.

So far this season <Faux:player#481> has 6 wins and 10 losses and a 4.25 ERA.
