MiLPBE Names Best Player
19-year-old <Jake Grinz:player#472> made a few <Anchorage:team#16> headlines during this past week as the rookie second baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Minor League PBE officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 17 at-bats to put up a .706 average. Grinz collected no home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

This year <Grinz:player#472> is batting .353 with 8 home runs. He has scored 79 runs and driven in 57.
