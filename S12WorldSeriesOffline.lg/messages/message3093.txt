Fans of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> are certainly optimistic regarding their team's chances to become World Series champions, after it became official that the club has won the Pro Baseball Experience Western Division.

It would be the 2nd league championship for the club, which has won the Pro Baseball Experience Western Division for the 5th time.

The team manager said he's spent many years in pro ball, but there's "just something special" about his current group of players.
