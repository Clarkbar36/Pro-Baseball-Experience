Springfield Hits 2 home runs in Wheelers Win
The Anchorage Wheelers turned back the Amarillo Armadillos, 5-4, at Balto Memorial Field at The Igloo. Smitty Springfield was named player of the game for his fine performance. He was 2 for 4 with 2 home runs, while adding 4 RBI and scoring twice. Amp Dupree, credited with the win, is now 10-6. He allowed 1 run on 7 hits in 7 innings. The first-place Wheelers have their sights set on making a deep playoff run.

Had Desmond Andrews not delivered a key base hit in the bottom of the seventh, the outcome might have been different. Instead, with two down and runners on 1st and 2nd, Andrews hit a run-scoring single. That made the score 2-1, in favor of the Wheelers.

"I'm just happy to help the team win," Springfield told the Anchorage Gazette.
