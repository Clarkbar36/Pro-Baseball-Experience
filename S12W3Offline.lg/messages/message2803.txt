PBE Announces Best Batter Award
<Paulo Di Stephano:player#48> battered Pro Baseball Experience pitchers all October and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around Pro Baseball Experience long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 29-year-old Di Stephano told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 17 games, Di Stephano hit .393 with 6 home runs, 20 RBIs and scored 21 runs.

Thus far this year <Di Stephano:player#48> has hit .308 with 26 home runs and 83 RBIs.
