Wheelers Topple Armadillos with Late Rally
Amarillo left fielder Juan Dong was sensational. He was 2 for 3 with a home run, a triple, and a walk, while adding 4 RBI and scoring once, but it wasn't enough to rally his team to a win. Anchorage beat the Armadillos, 6-5. Dong was named the player of the game despite the loss. Nate Pearson earned the save for Anchorage, his 5th of the year. Anchorage, now 7-2, has gotten their season off to a good start.

Thomas Trey Oudekerk's hit in the top of the ninth was a game-changer. With one out, he rapped a sinker from Richard Johnson for a run-scoring double. It put Anchorage in front, 6-5.

"You can't get too high when things go well, or too low when things go bad. Right now, I'm just happy we got the win," Oudekerk told the Anchorage Gazette.
