Sloths Even With Space Rangers After Extra-Innings Win
The San Antonio Sloths went the distance and then some at USSSA Space Coast Coliseum today to drag themselves back into a tie with the Florida Space Rangers.

It took 10 innings to settle Game 2 of the Round 1, but San Antonio eventually took the 3-1 win behind the efforts of starting pitcher and player of the game Khamati Kahn. Kahn threw 7 innings of shutout ball to aid the San Antonio cause. The clubs are now tied 1-1 in their best-of-5 series.

Kahn allowed no earned runs on 3 hits over 7.1 innings for the Sloths. He walked 1 and struck out 8.

"I found a nice groove out there," said Kahn.

The next game is scheduled for Sunday in Providence. The scheduled starters for the game are Penny Kowers for the Florida Space Rangers and Chaz LaLocke for the the San Antonio Sloths.
