Koehn, Mounties Sink Wheelers Again in Game 2
The Kingston Mounties moved to within one game of the Rookies Finals today, downing the visiting Anchorage Wheelers at The Barracks by a score of 9-3. The win gives the Mounties a 2-0 advantage in the best-of-5 Rookies Round 1.

Sean Shea, who is the 10th-best prospect in the Mounties organization, chipped in for Kingston with a solo home run in the bottom of the fifth. For the game, the shortstop had 3 hits in 4 at-bats with 1 home run, 2 singles, 1 run scored, and 2 runs batted in.

"We're one game closer to our goal," Kingston manager said later. "It isn't any more complicated than that."

Game 3 is set to be played Friday. Slated to start are Drew Buckley for the Kingston Mounties and Tony Ford for the the Anchorage Wheelers.
