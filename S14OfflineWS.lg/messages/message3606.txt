Amarillo Catcher MiLPBE's Best Batter in July
<Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> fans believe they've got a talented young star in <Tom Ato:player#727>.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in July, as he hit .438 with 2 home runs, 15 RBIs and 12 runs scored.

The end result was the catcher being named the Minor League PBE Batter of the Month.

Thus far this year Ato is hitting .350 with 4 home runs, 29 RBIs and 28 runs scored.
