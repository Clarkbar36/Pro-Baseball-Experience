Red-Hot Di Stephano PBE Player of the Week
At the age of 32, <Paulo Di Stephano:player#48> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Death Valley:team#10> right fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .500 batting average. For his efforts <Di Stephano:player#48> collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 11 hits in 22 at-bats, 3 home runs and 6 RBIs last week in 6 games.

So far this year Di Stephano is batting .273 with 18 home runs, 69 RBIs and 81 runs scored.
