MiLPBE Starter of the Month Goes to Jimbo 
There's something about a young rookie winning the Minor League PBE Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with <DingDing Jimbo:player#736>, a 14-year-old starter for the <State College Swift Steeds :team#18>.

In September, he topped off the month with a 4-0 mark and 2.08 ERA in 5 starts, tossed 30.1 innings, fanned 15 and held opposing teams to a .277 average.

His record for the year is 6-4 in 14 starts and <Jimbo:player#736> has a 4.42 ERA.
