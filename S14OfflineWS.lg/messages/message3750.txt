Cancun's Tentacion Bags PBE Batting Award
At only 24 years of age, <Jahseh Tentacion:player#419> could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the <Cancun Toros:team#26>, as October was one to remember for the catcher and he has captured the Pro Baseball Experience Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .340 with 5 home runs, 12 RBIs and 13 runs scored.

For the season <Tentacion:player#419> is currently batting .249 with 17 home runs and 54 RBIs. In 100 games he has reached base at a .298 rate and scored 47 runs to date.
