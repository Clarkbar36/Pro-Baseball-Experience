MiLPBE Names Dynamos Rivers Winner
So far in his young career, <Erik Rivers:player#618> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old <Dallas Dynamos:team#29> second baseman hit at a torrid .450 clip as he collected 9 hits in 20 at-bats, 1 home run, 8 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

Presently <Rivers:player#618> is batting .289 and has collected 13 home runs, 64 RBIs, .354 OBP and scored 42 runs.
