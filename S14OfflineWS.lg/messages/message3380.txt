Adler Wins MiLPBE Player of the Week Trophy
22-year-old <Leon Adler:player#607> made a few <Amarillo:team#19> headlines during this past week as the rookie shortstop proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Minor League PBE officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .478 average. Adler collected 2 home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

Thus far this year Adler is batting .280 with 7 home runs, 54 RBIs and 38 runs scored.
