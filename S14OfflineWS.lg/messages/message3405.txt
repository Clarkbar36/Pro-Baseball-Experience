Division Remains Up for Grabs, but Amarillo in Postseason
Postseason baseball is coming to Amarillo.

The <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> have punched their ticket past the regular season, by today securing a playoff berth on the strength of their 53-43 record.

The  West Division club can now focus on trying to win the division, but mathematically the club can no longer be ousted from a playoff spot.
