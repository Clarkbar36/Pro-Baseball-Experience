MiLPBE's Best Player, Brinkley of Amarillo
23-year-old <Harvey Brinkley:player#621> made a few <Amarillo:team#19> headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Minor League PBE officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 20 at-bats to put up a .550 average. Brinkley collected 1 home run, 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

Thus far this season in 65 games Brinkley is batting .275 with 6 home runs, 29 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has 68 hits in 247 at-bats.
