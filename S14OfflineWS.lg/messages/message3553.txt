Kingston's Sandberg MiLPBE Star of the Week
So far in his young career, <Robert Sandberg:player#571> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old <Kingston Mounties:team#17> first baseman hit at a torrid .588 clip as he collected 10 hits in 17 at-bats, 2 home runs, 2 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

This year <Sandberg:player#571> is batting .293 with 7 home runs and 9 RBIs.
