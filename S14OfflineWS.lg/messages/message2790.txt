Space Rangers Win Tight Pro Baseball Experience Eastern Division Race
Fans of the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> have a lot to get excited about, as the club has captured the Pro Baseball Experience Eastern Division crown.

It's the 2nd time in franchise history that the club has finished atop the standings and they now have an opportunity to capture the franchise's 1st Pro Baseball Experience championship.

The team manager said there's "a lot of excitement" in Florida over the club right now and they hope to live up to expectations.

For his part, starting pitcher <D'Aroldis Borkman:player#62> said the division championship is "just a start" for his team.

"We want to go all the way, win the World Series," he said. "Winning the division - we could get used to this."
