PBE All Star Game a Hit with Fans
Today Nielsen announced the ratings for PBE's annual All Star game and the long and short of it was - it was a hit. "This year we saw enormous growth in the number of homes viewing the game, said Pro Baseball Experience's Commissioner Dan Rawlings. He continue, "And to tell you the truth, we're not surprised as there really is no better sport in the world. The number of players we have in the league right now that are exceptional, not only for this period of baseball history but for the entire length of the sport's history is beyond compare."
