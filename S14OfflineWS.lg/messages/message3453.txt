Love Honored by MiLPBE as 2029 Best Rookie
<James Love:player#625> put together a sparkling rookie season to snag the Minor League PBE's Rookie of the Year Award for 2029.

In 100 games, the <State College:team#18> second baseman hit .323 with 125 hits, 19 home runs, 74 RBIs, 42 walks, 64 runs scored and a .393 on-base percentage.

<Yong-sun Pak:player#606> of the <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> finished second in voting, while <Izzy D'Alessio:player#612> of the <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> finished third.
