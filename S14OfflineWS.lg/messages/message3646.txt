Wheelers Forty-Three Wins MiLPBE Best Hurler Honor
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask <Jose Forty-Three:player#462>, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> reliever, 20, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .237 average and walked away with the Minor League PBE Pitcher of the Month award for August.

Forty-Three posted a 4-0 mark and a 1.82 ERA in no relief appearances. In 24.2 innings, he had no saves, 16 strikeouts, and 6 walks.

This season <Forty-Three:player#462> has made no relief appearances, recorded no saves and compiled a 8-5 won-lost mark and a 3.14 ERA. In pitching 97.1 innings, he has totaled 67 strikeouts and 39 walks.
