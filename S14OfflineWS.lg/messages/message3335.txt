Nawx Wins MiLPBE Batter of the Month
<Revan Nawx:player#637> made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Minor League PBE this past month.

The <Kansas City Hepcats:team#28> second baseman shined through the month of August and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 23-year-old hit .468 with 2 home runs, 15 RBIs and 12 runs scored.

This year <Nawx:player#637> has hit at a .326 clip with 87 hits, 3 home runs, 31 RBIs and 30 runs scored.
