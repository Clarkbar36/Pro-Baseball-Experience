Bailey Wins MiLPBE Honor
25-year-old <Betty Bailey:player#459> made a few <Kingston:team#17> headlines during this past week as the rookie catcher proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Minor League PBE officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 10 hits in 18 at-bats to put up a .556 average. Bailey collected 2 home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

Thus far this year Bailey is hitting .307 with 10 home runs, 48 RBIs and 37 runs scored.
