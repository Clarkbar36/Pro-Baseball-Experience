Voyagers Out of Time, Out of Playoffs
The <New York Voyagers:team#1> will have to put any fleeting championship aspirations on hold for the time being. The Voyagers were mathematically eliminated from contention for the 2030 playoffs today.

Before this season, the Voyagers last missed the playoffs in 2027. In the 13 years they have been a Pro Baseball Experience franchise, they have missed the playoffs 10 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 8 times. New York last won the World Series in 2028.
