Minor League PBE's Best Batter Announced
So far in his young career, <Toby McMuffin:player#620> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> left fielder hit at a torrid .417 clip as he collected 10 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

In 57 games this year McMuffin has posted a .341 average and put up these numbers: 7 home runs, 34 RBIs and 37 runs scored.
