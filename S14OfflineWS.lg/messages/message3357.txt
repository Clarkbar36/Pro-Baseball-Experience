PBE Names Best Player
At the age of 29, <Kimetrius  Webster :player#24> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Florida:team#24> first baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .353 batting average. For his efforts <Webster :player#24> collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 6 hits in 17 at-bats, 3 home runs and 6 RBIs last week in 5 games.

In the current season, <Webster :player#24> has put up a .239 batting average with 68 hits, 17 home runs, 54 RBI and scored 43 runs.
