PBE Names Rookie of the Month
21-year-old <Amp Dupree:player#529> is deceptively poised for such a young man. In 3 starts he bagged 3 wins in October and took home the Pro Baseball Experience Rookie of the Month Award.

The <Utah:team#25> hurler logged a 3-0 record, 13 strikeouts in 19.1 innings, held opponents to a .225 average and compiled an ERA of 1.86.

So far this year <Dupree:player#529> has made 25 starts, tossed 145.1 innings, struck out 111 batters and walked 49. His record is 8-14 with a 6.07 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .280 batting average.
