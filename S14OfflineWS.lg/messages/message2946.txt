Blaze Collects Player of the Week Award
At the age of 33, <Johnny Blaze:player#537> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Amarillo:team#19> first baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .444 batting average. For his efforts <Blaze:player#537> collected the Minor League PBE Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 12 hits in 27 at-bats, 2 home runs and 5 RBIs last week in 6 games.

In 33 games this year Blaze has posted a .380 average and put up these numbers: 7 home runs, 24 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
