Flowers Earns MiLPBE's Best Pitcher Honor
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask <Calloway Flowers:player#595>, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> reliever, 19, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .232 average and walked away with the Minor League PBE Pitcher of the Month award for October.

Flowers posted a 3-0 mark and a 3.46 ERA in no relief appearances. In 26 innings, he had no saves, 14 strikeouts, and 9 walks.

<Flowers:player#595> has earned no saves and a 8-8 mark this season with a 3.73 earned run average in no relief appearances. He has worked 169 innings, yielded 158 hits, struck out 99 batters and walked 59.
