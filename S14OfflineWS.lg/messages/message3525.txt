Jimmy Dong Selected May's Best Batter
<Jimmy Dong:player#15> battered Pro Baseball Experience pitchers all May and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around Pro Baseball Experience long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 32-year-old Dong told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 12 games, Dong hit .360 with 4 home runs, 14 RBIs and scored 16 runs.
