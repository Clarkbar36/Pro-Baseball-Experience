Alex Selich Captures MiLPBE Award
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Minor League PBE, but last week <Alex Selich:player#553> of the <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week.
