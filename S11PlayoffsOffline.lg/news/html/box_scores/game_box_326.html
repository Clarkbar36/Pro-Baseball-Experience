Wheelers Slip Past State College, 4-3
The Anchorage Wheelers did just enough to sneak past the State College Swift Steeds , 4-3, at Balto Memorial Field at The Igloo. Anchorage pitcher Alan Abel earned player of the game honors. Anchorage reliever Hisoka Ito picked up the win to move to 1-0. Twain Teetoo suffered the loss. Anchorage, now 8-4, has played well in the early going.

The Wheelers got a big at-bat from third baseman Kris Kramer. For the game, Kramer went 3-5 with 3 singles, while adding 3 RBI. In the bottom of the 11th, with the bases loaded, he stroked a run-scoring single. His 20th hit of the season gave Anchorage a 4-3 lead.

"If we play like this, we can win a lot of games," Abel told the Anchorage News.
