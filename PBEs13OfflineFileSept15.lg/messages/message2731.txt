Division Remains Up for Grabs, but Death Valley in Postseason
The fate of the Pro Baseball Experience Western Division remains officially undecided, but one thing is for sure - the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> are heading to the playoffs.

With a 57-18 mark, Death Valley officially secured a place in this year's postseason. The club will now wait to see where they end up seeded in the postseason - as a division or wild card winner.
