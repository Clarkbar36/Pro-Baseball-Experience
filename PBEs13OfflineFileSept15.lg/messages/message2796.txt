Playoff Bound: State College Secures Wild Card
Team owner Chad Powers said he was "proud and excited" for his players and coaches, after the <State College Swift Steeds :team#18> officially secured a playoff berth.

"This is what you play for, to try to bring a championship to State College," he said. "The guys are well on their way."

The  East Division club's record currently sits at 52-48.
