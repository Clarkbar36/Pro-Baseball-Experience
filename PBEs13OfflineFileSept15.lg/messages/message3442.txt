Scorpions KO Space Rangers
It's party time in Death Valley. The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 2029 World Series today. They defeated the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> 5-2 at HeatWave Park to officially wrap up the series 4-1.

Asked how it feels to be champion, center fielder <Jimmy Dong:player#15> was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."
