PBE Gives Outstanding Pitcher Award
The 2028 Pro Baseball Experience Pitcher of the Year Award winner is <D'Aroldis Borkman:player#62> of the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24>.

He started 25 games and compiled a 14-5 record with an ERA of 2.30 in 164.2 innings with 178 strikeouts.

<Guy Walker:player#12> of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> finished second in voting, while <Elston Sinclair:player#208> of the <New York Voyagers:team#1> finished third.
