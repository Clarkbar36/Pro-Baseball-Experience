MiLPBE Presents Player of the Week Trophy
So far in his young career, <Mac Smart:player#558> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> second baseman hit at a torrid .526 clip as he collected 10 hits in 19 at-bats, 1 home run, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

This year <Smart:player#558> has socked 75 hits in 244 at-bats for a .307 average with 7 home runs. He is getting on base at a .352 pace and his totals also include 32 RBIs and 33 runs scored.
