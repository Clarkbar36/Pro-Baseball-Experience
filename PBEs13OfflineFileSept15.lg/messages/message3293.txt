MiLPBE Player of the Week Goes to Mardukas of State College
So far in his young career, <Hank Mardukas:player#539> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old <State College Swift Steeds :team#18> first baseman hit at a torrid .533 clip as he collected 8 hits in 15 at-bats, 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

For the season <Mardukas:player#539> has hit .302 with 4 home runs and 25 RBIs. He has played in 52 games and scored 31 times.
