MiLPBE Awards Player of the Week Trophy
<Dandy Chiggins Jr.:player#543> has been in a real groove lately. The 29-year-old <Kingston Mounties:team#17> left fielder nabbed Minor League PBE Player of the Week honors today after beating up on opposing pitchers all last week.

Chiggins Jr.'s hot streak featured 11 hits in 18 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs, 7 runs scored and a .611 average.
