MacKaye Jr. Injured Trying to Unjam Garage Door
Providence starting pitcher <Benton MacKaye Jr.:player#556> will be out of service for approximately 2 weeks after suffering a bruised wrist. The injury occurred as MacKaye Jr. was reportedly attempting to unjam his garage door. X-rays showed no signs of breakage, but according to team medical staff, there is a significant bone bruise. When reached for comment, MacKaye Jr. simply replied, "It was just a matter of carelessness on my part. I'm going to rest it for now and I should be back in action in a couple of weeks."
