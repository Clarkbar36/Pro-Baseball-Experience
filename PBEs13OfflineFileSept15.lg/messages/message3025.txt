Kingston's Chiggins Jr. Collects August Batting Honor
<Dandy Chiggins Jr.:player#543> has a lot going for him. The <Kingston:team#17> right fielder just broke into the majors this year and now the Minor League PBE has just picked him as its top hitter for August.

In 14 games last month this outstanding rookie batted .350 with 7 home runs, 17 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Batting .315 to date, Chiggins Jr. has 15 home runs and 56 RBIs.
