Best MiLPBE Reliever Named
<Brent Lomew:player#552> said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 23 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> rising star has won the Minor League PBE Reliever of the Year Award for 2028.

He captured the award on the strength of his 2.17 ERA over 74.2 innings. He also had 6 saves in 44 relief appearances, 58 strikeouts and only 17 walks.

<Nate Pearson:player#533> of the <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> finished second in voting, while <Richard Johnsson:player#580> of the <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> finished third.
