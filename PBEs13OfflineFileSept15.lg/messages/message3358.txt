MiLPBE Announces Player of the Week
So far in his young career, <Decio Penna:player#632> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 16-year-old <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> third baseman hit at a torrid .474 clip as he collected 9 hits in 19 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

This year <Penna:player#632> has collected a .298 batting average to date with 90 hits in 302 at-bats, 41 RBIs, 7 home runs and scored 39 times. He has played in 76 games.
