PBE Presents Batter of Month Trophy to Storm 
<Louis  Storm :player#146> has been the model of consistency over the past month and has been a tough out, claiming the Pro Baseball Experience Batter of the Month trophy for September.

The <New York:team#1> second baseman hit .410 with 5 home runs, 21 RBIs and 18 runs scored. He was also walked 11 times and posted a .479 on-base percentage. 

So far this season Storm  is hitting .300 with 11 home runs, 67 RBIs and 56 runs scored. He has 105 hits in 350 at-bats and a .363 OBP.
