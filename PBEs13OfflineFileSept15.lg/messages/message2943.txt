Oudekerk Crowned Top MiLPBE Hitter in June
For a rookie just breaking in, <Thomas Trey Oudekerk:player#508> certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the MiLPBE Batter of the Month award for June.

The 22-year-old first baseman for the <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> hit .362 with 4 home runs, 17 RBIs and 19 runs scored.
