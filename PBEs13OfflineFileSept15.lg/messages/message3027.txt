Di Stephano Picked PBE Player of the Week
At the age of 30, <Paulo Di Stephano:player#48> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Death Valley:team#10> shortstop put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .409 batting average. For his efforts <Di Stephano:player#48> collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 9 hits in 22 at-bats, 2 home runs and 8 RBIs last week in 5 games.

Batting .292 to date, Di Stephano has 16 home runs and 58 RBIs.
