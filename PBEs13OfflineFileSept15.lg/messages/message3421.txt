MiLPBE News: Regular Season ends
Get ready for some baseball! The playoffs are here! For those clubs fortunate enough to make the postseason, baseball's second season is just beginning. But for the majority of players and teams, today is the first day of a long offseason. Clubs in the playoffs have begun preparation for their upcoming showdowns to determine the 2029 Minor League PBE champions. The best teams will go at it "tooth and nail" for the Rookies Finals crown and it all gets started in just a few days.
