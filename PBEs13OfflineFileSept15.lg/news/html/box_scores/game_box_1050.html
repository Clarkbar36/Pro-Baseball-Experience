Aviators Even With Vandals After Extra-Innings Win
The Outer Banks Aviators went the distance and then some at Vandal Dome today to drag themselves back into a tie with the Vancouver Vandals.

It took 12 innings to settle Game 2 of the Wild Card Round, but Outer Banks eventually took the 8-5 win behind the efforts of starting pitcher and player of the game Johnathon Livingston. Livingston threw 6 innings of 2-hit ball to aid the Outer Banks cause. The clubs are now tied 1-1 in their best-of-5 series.

Outer Banks used the timely hitting of Kenny McCormick to capture the win. The shortstop stroked a 2-run double in the top of the 12th inning to put the Aviators on top, 6-4. It was his only hit in 3 at-bats, but he made it count.

"The boys were really swinging the bats today," winning manager said.

The next game is scheduled for Friday at The Island. Slated to start are Greg Steiner for Vancouver and Mookie Banks for Outer Banks.
