Hunter Harrison Wins PBE Trophy
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. <Hunter Harrison:player#375>, the <Vancouver Vandals:team#11> 25-year-old third baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 14 hits in 29 at-bats, compiling a .483 average with 1 home run and 3 RBIs.

This season <Harrison:player#375> is batting .331 with 10 home runs. His totals include 45 RBIs and 68 runs scored.
