MiLPBE Player of the Week Goes to Bauer of Kansas City
So far in his young career, <Anthony Bauer:player#563> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 20-year-old <Kansas City Hepcats:team#28> left fielder hit at a torrid .435 clip as he collected 10 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

This season <Bauer:player#563> is batting .275 with 8 home runs. His totals include 27 RBIs and 24 runs scored.
