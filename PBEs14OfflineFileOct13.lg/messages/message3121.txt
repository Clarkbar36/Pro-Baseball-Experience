San Antonio Win Round 1
The <San Antonio Sloths:team#9> are off to the World Series.

"Two great teams, only one can go on and we're happy that's us," said <Vick Thunder:player#270>, who was named series MVP. "There's no easy games, no easy series in the Pro Baseball Experience playoffs."
