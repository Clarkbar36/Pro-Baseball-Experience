Kingston Starter Named MiLPBE's #1 Pitcher
The Minor League PBE named the winner of the Pitcher of the Month Award.
It went to <Colton Kai:player#584> of <Kingston Mounties:team#17>, who impressed the voters with a glittering 3-0 record in August.

The <Kingston:team#17> star hurler made 4 starts, allowed 18 hits in 24.2 innings, struck out 13 and had a 1.09 ERA.

Checking out his stats for the season, <Kai:player#584> has racked up 8 wins and 8 losses in 22 starts, 3.72 ERA and struck out 91 batters in 133 innings.
