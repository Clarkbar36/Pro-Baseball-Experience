PBE Announces Top Player Award
<Paulo Di Stephano:player#48> has been around Pro Baseball Experience enough to know a good hot streak when he sees one -- and he got to see one firsthand all year. For his standout run through the 2027 season, he's taking home the Most Valuable Player Award in the Pro Baseball Experience.

The <Death Valley:team#10> shortstop put up a batting average of .308 with 33 doubles, 3 triples, 26 home runs and 83 RBIs.

<Kevin Biggio:player#268> of the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> finished second in voting, while <Weldon Borthwell:player#29> of the <New York Voyagers:team#1> finished third.
