PBE News: Spring Training Begins
Grass and chalk. Leather and pine tar. These are the scents of baseball. Fans are flocking to training camps across Pro Baseball Experience where spring training is just getting under way. Many tough decisions rest before team coaches and scouts. Is it time to take a chance on the raw youngster? Does that veteran have one more productive season left? The choices made in the coming weeks could determine the outcome of the season ahead.
