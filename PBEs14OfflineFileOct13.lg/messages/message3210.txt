PBE #1 Pitcher in May is Death Valley's Walker
<Guy Walker:player#12>'s been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 3-0 in May, the 29-year-old <Death Valley:team#10> hurler has been named Pro Baseball Experience Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 28 in 22.2 innings, logged an ERA of 1.19 and allowed opponents to hit .188 against him.
