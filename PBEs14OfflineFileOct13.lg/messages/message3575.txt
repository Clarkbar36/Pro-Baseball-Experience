Big Bat Pays Off, McDonald Player of the Week
22-year-old <Bobby McDonald:player#629> made a few <Kingston:team#17> headlines during this past week as the rookie first baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Minor League PBE officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 10 hits in 22 at-bats to put up a .455 average. McDonald collected 3 home runs, 6 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

For the season <McDonald:player#629> is hitting .322, batted in 19 runs with 5 home runs and 30 runs scored.
