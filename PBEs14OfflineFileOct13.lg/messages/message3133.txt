Faux Captures 2028 Rookie of the Year Award
<Blake Faux:player#481>'s age didn't show very often in his first year. He performed more like a veteran on the hill for the <Utah Railroaders:team#25> and today he walked away with the 2028 Pro Baseball Experience Rookie of the Year Award.

The 22-year-old starter registered a 5-12 record this season in 24 starts. Over 160.2 innings he gave up 158 hits, struck out 118 and issued 56 walks as he held the opposition to a .255 batting average and fashioned a 4.37 ERA.

<Mark Taylor:player#527> of the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24> finished second in voting, while <Detective Wooloo:player#456> of the <Providence Crabs:team#3> finished third.
