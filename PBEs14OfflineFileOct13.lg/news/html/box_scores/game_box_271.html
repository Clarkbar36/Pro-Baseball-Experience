Thunder, Toros Clobber Sloths
Conditions were not ideal but Cancun and San Antonio were able to put their game in the books.

The Cancun Toros topped the San Antonio Sloths, 13-2, in Pro Baseball Experience action at The Treehouse. The player of the game was Cancun left fielder Vick Thunder. Esteban Rodriguez was the winner. Over 7.2 innings, he allowed 2 runs on 4 hits, with no walks and 11 strikeouts.

Thunder had a big at-bat with runners on 1st and 2nd in the top of the fifth inning, when he hit a 3-run home run. The Toros went on to win.

"One win at a time, one game at a time. We try not to get too far ahead of ourselves," Thunder told the Cancun Observer.

The 4th-place Toros now have a win-loss record of 29-25.
