Aviators Come Back Late to Clip Vandals
The Outer Banks Aviators and Vancouver Vandals obliged those fans who came to The Island hoping for an exciting game.

The Outer Banks Aviators took Vancouver pitcher Peter Dawson's best shot and still were able to claim a win at The Island. The final score had Outer Banks getting past the Vandals, 5-4. Dawson went 6 innings and allowed 2 earned runs, striking out 6. Pete Rose, credited with the win, is now 3-0. He allowed no runs on no hits in 1.1 innings. Falco Von Adler converted his 4th save in 6 tries.

A crucial at-bat came with runners on 2nd and 3rd and two out in the bottom of the seventh inning. That's when designated hitter Toshihiro Ishikawa, who is a four-time Gold Glove Award winner, drew a walk. The Aviators went on to win.

"If we play like this, we can win a lot of games," Ishikawa told the Outer Banks Daily News.

The Aviators bumped their record to 42-33 with this latest triumph.
