Week's Premier Player: New York's Borthwell
<Weldon Borthwell:player#29> demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

The <New York:team#1> third baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .421 average (8-for-19) with 4 home runs and 11 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.
