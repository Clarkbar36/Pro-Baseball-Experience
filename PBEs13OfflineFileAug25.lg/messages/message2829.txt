Styles Named Top Reliever for MiLPBE
<Harry Styles:player#504> said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 21 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the <State College Swift Steeds :team#18> rising star has won the Minor League PBE Reliever of the Year Award for 2027.

He captured the award on the strength of his 2.98 ERA over 87.2 innings. He also had 2 saves in 52 relief appearances, 90 strikeouts and only 31 walks.

<Nate Pearson:player#533> of the <Anchorage Wheelers:team#16> finished second in voting, while <Colby Kidd:player#505> of the <Kingston Mounties:team#17> finished third.
