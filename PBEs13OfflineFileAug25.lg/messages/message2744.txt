Week's #1 Player: Amarillo's Dong
So far in his young career, <Juan Dong:player#484> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> first baseman hit at a torrid .600 clip as he collected 9 hits in 15 at-bats, 1 home run, 4 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

This year <Dong:player#484> is hitting .283 with 5 home runs and 31 RBIs in 61 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .352 with 20 walks and 33 runs scored.
