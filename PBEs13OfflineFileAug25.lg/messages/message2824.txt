2027 Best Newcomer is Providence's Payton
<A.C. Payton:player#465>'s age didn't show very often in his first year. He performed more like a veteran on the hill for the <Providence Crabs:team#3> and today he walked away with the 2027 Pro Baseball Experience Rookie of the Year Award.

The 22-year-old starter registered a 6-10 record this season in 25 starts. Over 139.1 innings he gave up 161 hits, struck out 109 and issued 58 walks as he held the opposition to a .286 batting average and fashioned a 5.30 ERA.

<Hendrix Lamar:player#407> of the <Vancouver Vandals:team#11> finished second in voting, while <Ed Barker:player#458> of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> finished third.
