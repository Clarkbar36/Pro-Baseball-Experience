Space Rangers Webster  Named PBE's #1 Player
At the age of 29, <Kimetrius  Webster :player#24> might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The <Florida:team#24> first baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .474 batting average. For his efforts <Webster :player#24> collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 9 hits in 19 at-bats, 3 home runs and 5 RBIs last week in 5 games.

In 47 games this year Webster  has posted a .264 average and put up these numbers: 11 home runs, 37 RBIs and 28 runs scored.
