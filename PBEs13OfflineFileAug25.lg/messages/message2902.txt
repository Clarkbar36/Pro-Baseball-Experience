PBE Starter of the Month Goes to Borkman 
Veteran <D'Aroldis Borkman:player#62> put together quite a successful stretch in May for the <Florida Space Rangers:team#24>, earning himself the Pro Baseball Experience Pitcher of the Month award.

Borkman racked up a 3-0 record in 3 starts, posting a 0.79 ERA over 22.2 innings and striking out 26.
