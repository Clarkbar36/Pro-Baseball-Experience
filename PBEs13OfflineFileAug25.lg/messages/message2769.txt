Vancouver's Bellinger PBE's Best
So far in his young career, <Kyle Bellinger:player#409> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Pro Baseball Experience in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old <Vancouver Vandals:team#11> second baseman hit at a torrid .533 clip as he collected 8 hits in 15 at-bats, 3 home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

<Bellinger:player#409> is batting .241 with 11 home runs and 41 RBIs this season.
