Minor League PBE Names Love Best Batter
So far in his young career, <James Love:player#625> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 17-year-old <State College Swift Steeds :team#18> second baseman hit at a torrid .429 clip as he collected 9 hits in 21 at-bats, 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

Thus far in 2029 Love is hitting .378 with 8 home runs, 30 RBIs, 17 runs scored and has compiled a .440 OBP.
