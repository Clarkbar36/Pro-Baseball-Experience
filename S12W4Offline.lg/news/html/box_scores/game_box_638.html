Thrilling Walk-Off Win for Scorpions
The HeatWave Park faithful were treated to a thrilling, walk-off win.

The Death Valley Scorpions eked out a 8-7 win versus the Utah Railroaders at HeatWave Park. Nobody did more than shortstop Paulo Di Stephano, who captured player of the game honors. The Scorpions bumped their record to 52-27 with this latest triumph.

Di Stephano had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the ninth. On a 1-1 changeup from Colben Fairborn, he banged a 2-run home run to right, giving the Scorpions a 8-7 lead. It was the 21st home run of the year for Di Stephano.

Di Stephano told a Death Valley Observer reporter that he was "satisfied" with the win.
