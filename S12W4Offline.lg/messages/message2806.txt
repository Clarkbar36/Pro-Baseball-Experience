Gonzales Snares MiLPBE Top Batter Award in October
Even though he is a newcomer to Minor League PBE, <Steve Gonzales:player#449> put up some good numbers in October and earned the MiLPBE Batter of the Month award.

He was one of <Anchorage:team#16>'s offensive leaders with a .373 average in 17 games. He also racked up 7 home runs, 18 RBIs, and 20 runs scored.

So far this year Gonzales is batting .309 with 19 home runs, 56 RBIs and 89 runs scored.
