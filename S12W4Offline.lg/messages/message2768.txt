Death Valley Wins Pro Baseball Experience Western Division Crown
The <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> stood out amongst the competition in the Pro Baseball Experience Western Division this season and can now lay claim to having officially clinched the division title.

It is the 4th time the club has won its division and they will be vying for the 2nd World Series in franchise history.

"Proud of everyone in the room," is how starting pitcher <Victoria Secret:player#227> described his feeling after the clinch. "We're number one now, but we need to make sure it stays that way when the playoffs are said and done."
