Dynamos Walk Off with Win
People near Perpetual Park no doubt heard the roar of the crowd as the winning run crossed the plate.

Dallas beat Kingston at Perpetual Park, eking out a 6-5 win. The win was due in large part to catcher Lucio O Cuaig, who earned player of the game honors. Dallas reliever Domingo Pozo picked up the win, upping his record to 2-1. Throwing Dude shouldered the loss.

O Cuaig had a game-changing single in the bottom of the ninth. On a full count changeup from Throwing Dude, he banged a 2-run single to left, giving the Dynamos a 6-5 lead. It was the 35th hit of the year for O Cuaig.

"We played hard and won," said O Cuaig. "Sometimes, baseball really is that easy.'

The Dynamos, at 16-12, lead by one game in their division.
