Mounties Win in Glorious Fashion
The Kingston Mounties put the giddy home crowd in an even more jubilant mood after beating the Anchorage Wheelers in the season opener at The Barracks. Shea had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the 11th. On a full count fastball from Alexei Brusilov, he banged a 2-run home run to left, giving the Mounties a 5-3 lead. It was the 1st home run of the year for Shea.

"It's great when the crowd gets into it," Shea said.
