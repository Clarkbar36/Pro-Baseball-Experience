Road Win Lifts Amarillo to 1-0 Lead in RF
The Amarillo Armadillos faced a tough opponent in State College Swift Steeds right fielder Buddy Cooper, but did just enough to pull out a win in Game 1 of the Rookies Finals, which they now lead 1-0. The Armadillos won 6-4 at Ballpark of Meeker, despite the efforts of Cooper, who went 3 for 5 with a home run and 2 singles. He drove in 3 runs and scored once and was named player of the game.

Amarillo grabbed control in the top of the sixth. With one out and a runner on 1st, Decio Penna delivered a 2-run home run, to make it 5-3 in favor of the Armadillos.

"Win or lose, we pick each other up in this clubhouse," Penna told the Amarillo Daily News.

Game 2 is scheduled for tomorrow at Ballpark of Meeker. Slated to start are Calloway Flowers for the Amarillo Armadillos and Dude Manbro for the the State College Swift Steeds .
