PBE Announces Outstanding Batter Award
<John DeMars:player#379> may be much younger than other hitters in the Pro Baseball Experience, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in October to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 24-year-old <Florida:team#24> left fielder hit .348 with 7 home runs, 21 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

For the season <DeMars:player#379> has produced 129 hits in 396 at-bats for a .326 average with 27 home runs and gotten on base at a .382 clip.
