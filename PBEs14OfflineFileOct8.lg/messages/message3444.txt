Dong is Best In PBE
It's been a year of triumph for veteran <Jimmy Dong:player#15> of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> as he hit his way to the 2029 Pro Baseball Experience Most Valuable Player Award.

The 31-year-old veteran compiled a .331 average and .460 on-base percentage by putting together 120 hits, 41 doubles, 6 triples, 17 home runs and 83 walks. He also scored 72 times and drove in 79 runs.

<Paulo Di Stephano:player#48> of the <Death Valley Scorpions:team#10> finished second in voting, while <Billy Benson:player#20> of the <Outer Banks Aviators:team#2> finished third.
