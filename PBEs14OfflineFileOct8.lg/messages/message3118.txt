MiLPBE News: The Offseason Begins
The 2028 season has come to a close and the offseason has begun. Minor League PBE fans are all too willing to entertain fantastic trade scenarios or shoot the breeze about their team's prospects for next season. But they really don't like the offseason, because the offseason means waiting. That star player who might be snagged in a blockbuster deal won't don his new team's uniform, step into the box or hurl a fastball down the pipe for many months yet. The next months will provide a different sort of activity, though, as teams reassess and retool for the coming campaign.
