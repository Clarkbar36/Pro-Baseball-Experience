MiLPBE's Top Player Named
So far in his young career, <Nic Wilson:player#555> has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Minor League PBE in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 19-year-old <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> first baseman hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 12 hits in 24 at-bats, 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

Thus far this year <Wilson:player#555> has hit .341 with 21 home runs and 59 RBIs.
