Pitcher of the Year Award Announced, Winner is Besarionis dze Jughashvili
This year there was no one better on the mound than <Ioseb Besarionis dze Jughashvili:player#573>. Posting 10 wins and a 3.72 ERA was only part of the story for the <Amarillo:team#19> superstar. For his outstanding performance in 2029 he was chosen as the Minor League PBE Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

From start to finish Besarionis dze Jughashvili never let up. In 33 starts he recorded 10 wins and 10 losses, 172 innings, 119 strikeouts, 42 walks, yielded 178 hits, and permitted opposing batters to hit .268 against him.

<Dude Manbro:player#668> of the <State College Swift Steeds :team#18> finished second in voting, while <Yong-sun Pak:player#606> of the <Amarillo Armadillos:team#19> finished third.
