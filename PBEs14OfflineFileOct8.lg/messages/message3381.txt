Dallas's Cider Acclaimed Best MiLPBE Hitter
At only 22 years of age, <Dickson Cider:player#630> could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the <Dallas Dynamos:team#29>, as September was one to remember for the first baseman and he has captured the Minor League PBE Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .325 with 9 home runs, 22 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

This year <Cider:player#630> is hitting .294 with 16 home runs and 53 RBIs in 86 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .376 with 41 walks and 44 runs scored.
