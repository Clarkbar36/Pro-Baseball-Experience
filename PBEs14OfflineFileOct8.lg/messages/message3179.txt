MiLPBE News: Pre-Season Begins
One season yields to another...and it is a time when a young man's fancy turns to baseball. The Boys of the Diamond will soon be here in all their radiant glory to put some sparkle, purpose, and meaning back into the lives of baseball fans everywhere. There will be many of those seemingly meaningless exhibition games, but they do have an important purpose -- to prepare teams for the coming regular season. The 2029 Minor League PBE schedule has been announced, and the preseason starts today.

Please don't forget to set your scouting budget and player development budget during the pre-season!
