PBE Votes Scorpions Dinero Top Player
A battle-tested <Eduardo Dinero:player#81> declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 34-year-old <Death Valley:team#10> shortstop collected the Pro Baseball Experience Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .440 average, 11 hits in 25 at-bats, 4 home runs, 15 RBIs and 11 runs scored.
