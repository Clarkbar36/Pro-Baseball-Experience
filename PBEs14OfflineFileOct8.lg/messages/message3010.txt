Blaze Selected Top Player
Veteran <Johnny Blaze:player#537> had a great week at the plate for the <Mounties:team#17> to capture the majority of votes for Player of the Week in the Minor League PBE.

The 33-year-old Kingston first baseman built a .375 average on the strength of 9 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs and 7 RBIs.

<Blaze:player#537> has a .338 average and a .407 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 78 hits, 11 home runs and driven in 37 runs.
