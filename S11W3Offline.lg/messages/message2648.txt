PBE News: Trade Deadline
General managers are busy across Pro Baseball Experience as the trading deadline looms near. Clubs looking to pawn off that overpaid superstar or deal the sure-to-leave free agent will be putting their bargaining skills to the test as other clubs, hoping to snag a few young players prepare to receive a king's ransom. The intrigue, the mystery, the media camped at league headquarters to catch those down-to-the-wire deals, there is no greater day in baseball. Normal trades are allowed again once the playoffs are over and the offseason begins.
