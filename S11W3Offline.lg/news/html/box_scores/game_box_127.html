McBash 3-5, Space Rangers Move to 20-12
The first-place Florida Space Rangers picked up another win at USSSA Space Coast Coliseum.

Getting a good performance from third baseman George McBash, the Florida Space Rangers defeated the Vancouver Vandals, 8-5. McBash was 3-5 with a home run and a triple. He scored 2 runs and drove in 4 for the Space Rangers. Florida reliever Sebastian Musgrave-Smythe picked up the win, upping his record to 2-3. Alvin Enax shouldered the loss. Trent Boult closed the game out for Florida, earning his 4th save.

McBash had a game-changing home run in the bottom of the eighth. On a 0-2 screwball from Leo Komarov, he banged a 3-run home run to left-center, giving the Space Rangers a 8-5 lead. It was the 9th home run of the year for McBash.

"We'll put this win in our pocket and go after the next one," said McBash.

The Space Rangers bumped their record to 20-12 with this latest triumph.
