Multi-HR Day for DeMars as Space Rangers Overpower Aviators, 8-6
The Florida Space Rangers beat the Outer Banks Aviators, 8-6, at The Island. The win was largely due to the efforts of right fielder John DeMars, who captured player of the game honors. He hit 2 home runs. He drove in 5 runs and scored twice. Florida leads the Pro Baseball Experience Eastern Division by 2½ games.

DeMars contributed a big hit, a grand slam home run, for Florida in the top of the third. With his team trailing 6-3, DeMars took a fastball from Barry Keller and stroked the home run to right. His 6th home run of the season allowed the Space Rangers to leap in front, 7-6.

DeMars told a Florida Today reporter that he was "satisfied" with the win.
