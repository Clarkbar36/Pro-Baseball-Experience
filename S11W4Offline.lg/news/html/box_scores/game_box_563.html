Fazio Leads Swift Steeds to Win over Armadillos
The Dillo Dome was the site for Minor League PBE action featuring the Amarillo Armadillos and State College Swift Steeds .

A 5-RBI performance from the 18-year old second baseman Michael Fazio helped the State College Swift Steeds beat the Amarillo Armadillos, 8-4. State College reliever Harry Styles picked up the win, upping his record to 4-3. Drennifer Tarbington took the loss.

Had Jax Duggan not delivered a key base hit in the top of the eighth, the outcome might have been different. Instead, with two down and the bases loaded, Duggan hit a 2-run single. That made the score 5-4, in favor of the Swift Steeds .

Fazio called the triumph "a good team win."

State College leads the East Division by 8 games.
