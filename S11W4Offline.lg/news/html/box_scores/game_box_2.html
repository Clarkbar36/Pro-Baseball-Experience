Russell Slugs Space Rangers to 12-8 Win
The Florida Space Rangers started the 2027 season win an impressive 12-8 win against the Outer Banks Aviators at USSSA Space Coast Coliseum. Hawk Russell, who won the Rookie of the Year Award in 2020, was named player of the game. Trent Boult picked up the save, his 1st.

Russell contributed a big hit, a grand slam home run, for Florida in the bottom of the seventh. With his team trailing 8-5, Russell took a fastball from Pete Rose and stroked the home run to right. His 2nd home run of the season allowed the Space Rangers to leap in front, 9-8.

Russell called the triumph "a good team win."
